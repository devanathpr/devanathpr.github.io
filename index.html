About Me

I studied Sociology for my Bachelor's and Library & Information Science for my Master's. What began as a chance entry turned into a rewarding career — I graduated with a gold medal and have been working in academic libraries ever since.

I'm currently managing the library at ICTS-TIFR. My interests include technology adoption in libraries, sustainable practices, and quantitative science studies (scientometrics, research evaluation, and altmetrics).

Outside work, I enjoy running, badminton, following geopolitics, and reading widely.

Doctoral Research

I'm pursuing a part-time Ph.D. at Tumkur University on research productivity and the impact of Indian-funded research. My work uses quantitative methods to better understand how research performance is measured and what value it delivers.
