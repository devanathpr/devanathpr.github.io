About

I studied Sociology for my Bachelor's, then Library and Information Science for my Master's — not by choice, but by chance. That chance turned out very well: I graduated with a gold medal and built a career I deeply enjoy.

I currently manage the library at ICTS-TIFR, Bengaluru. My professional interests lie at the intersection of library science, technology, and sustainable practices. Outside work, I enjoy running, badminton, geopolitics, and reading widely.

Doctoral Research

I am pursuing a part-time Ph.D. at Tumkur University on research productivity and the impact of Indian-funded research — a quantitative study of how research performance is measured and what those measurements truly indicate.
