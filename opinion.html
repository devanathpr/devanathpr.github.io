Conferences

All of us enjoy attending conferences (atleast pretend to say), you get to explore places, meet new people, discuss your research.......

Lets talk about the conferences I went to present my work: they are predatory? I don't know what to name them! the conferences organised for library science (ASFIK in India ) are mostly Multidisciplinary and anything that is submitted will be accepted. So to bring confidence in poor scholars (like me) that they did amazing work and published something valuable!. There is no scope for Research discussions (at all), you get 5 mins to explain your work (with judges along the stage, they are to decide 'Best paper/presentation award'). Publication of the proceedings might take longer than you expect (sometimes more than a year also). To cut the long story short: we organise conferences for inclusion in assessments or rankings, that's all.
